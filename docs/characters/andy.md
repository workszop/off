# Andy

**Color:** terracotta `#D4765F` · **Sprites:** `assets/andy_idle.png`, `assets/andy_walk_right.png`

## Look

Balding, round wire glasses, warm closed-mouth smile, short-sleeve button
polo — the mild-mannered, slightly older office everyman.

## Personality

A bit cynical, but open — his humor runs on dry understatement rather than
punchlines ("I've been 'circling back' for so long I'm legally a
roundabout"), and he's quietly anxious about being caught slacking. None of
that closes him off: he's warm underneath it, easy to talk to, and openly
fond of his coworkers.

- **Likes the girls** — genuinely fond of Jazz and Olex, admiring rather
  than smooth about it ("I would follow her into a burning building.
  Calmly. Eventually.").
- **Juggler, slackliner** — a hidden athletic/circus-arts streak that
  contradicts his desk-bound, "admires his bike from the box" energy.
- **AI** — his newest favorite thing to have opinions about; treats every
  chatbot interaction as material.
- **Music** — the office's Rolling Stones devotee, cited with real
  specificity (Exile on Main St., Beggars Banquet).
- **Ghosts** — genuinely on the fence, not a believer but not dismissive;
  has a named "ghost" at home (Gerald).
- **Reading** — slow and sincere, stuck on the same book since January.
- **Habits** — slightly scruffy, mildly food-obsessed at low stakes
  (Tupperware wars, sad desk salads).

## Voice

Passive, wry, wants nobody to notice him in meetings ("So… are we
starting?"). Funniest when underselling something that clearly matters to
him.

## In-game mood words

`coping`, `peckish`, `contemplative`, `circling back`

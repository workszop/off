# Olex

**Color:** lavender `#A894C7` · **Sprites:** `assets/olex_idle.png`,
`assets/olex_walk_left.png`

## Look

Messy hair bun, round glasses, plain casual t-shirt — the classic
low-maintenance developer look.

## Personality

Dry, data-driven, allergic to unearned claims — the office skeptic and de
facto engineer, running everything through an analytical lens even when
it's absurd ("Veganism is statistically net positive. I have a
spreadsheet.").

- **Loves books, reads a lot** — a genuine, unrepentant reader (three
  books this month, a to-read pile she calls "a structural hazard"). Her
  older self-deprecating jokes about barely reading are exaggeration
  layered on top of a real habit, not the truth.
- **Happy about her life** — content and grounded underneath the deadpan;
  the flatness is a delivery style, not a mood. "I'm doing great,
  actually."
- **Ghosts** — committed rationalist, but self-aware about the limits of
  that stance ("There is no empirical evidence for ghosts. There is also
  no empirical evidence my pull request will get reviewed.").
- **Work identity** — lives inside developer-brain metaphors for
  everything, from tea to shrimp nervous systems.
- **Tastes** — precise and a little pedantic about small rituals (milk in
  first, a spreadsheet-backed take on lab-grown meat).

## Voice

Deadpan, faintly weary on the surface, funniest when treating trivial
things with total seriousness. The dry tone is a habit of speech, not a
sign she's unhappy — she'll say so directly if asked.

## In-game mood words

`compiling`, `skeptical`, `optimizing`, `low power`

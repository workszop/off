# Jazz

**Color:** sage green `#7BA38C` · **Sprites:** `assets/jazz_idle.png`,
`assets/jazz_walk_left.png`, `assets/jazz_walk_right.png`

## Look

Long, loose dark hair, bright wide-eyed expression, sleeveless top — the
most visually animated and expressive of the three.

## Personality

The office's social glue — organizes, hosts, bakes, remembers birthdays
nobody else tracks, and takes visible pride in keeping things running
("I organized the supply closet. You're welcome.").

- **Talks about her friends** — a full, active life outside work she brings
  in constantly: group chats, best-friend visits, a wedding playlist she's
  been trusted with.
- **Friendly, likes everyone** — no beef with anyone, warmly inclusive,
  makes friends with the delivery guy, matchmakes coworkers at the printer.
- **Passionate** — throws herself fully into whatever she's into, whether
  that's a tea ritual, a TikTok account, or a ghost tour.
- **Ghosts** — a true believer, and enthusiastic about it; needles Olex
  about being wrong before.
- **TikTok** — her main platform, multiple accounts "for different vibes."
- **Style** — fashion-forward and self-aware about it; thrift shopping as
  competitive sport.

## Voice

Chronically upbeat, a little chaotic, self-aware about her own intensity
("Mostly. Mostly mostly."). Funniest when she's proud of something nobody
asked for.

## In-game mood words

`sparkling`, `organized`, `plotting a surprise`, `cozy`

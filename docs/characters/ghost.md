# Ghost

**Color:** sky blue `#6AA3D8` · **Sprite:** `assets/ghost.png`

## Look

A simple, round-eyed, friendly outline — designed to be cute rather than
scary, comic relief rather than a horror element.

## Personality

Entirely vibes-based. Its whole vocabulary is variations on "uhu"
(`uhu`, `uhu?`, `UHU!`, `uhuuuu`, `UHU UHU`), with punctuation and
capitalization doing all the emotional work: a plain "uhu" is idle
chatter, "UHU?!" is alarm or surprise, "uhuuuu" is drawn-out and dreamy.
It has opinions about coffee and meetings just like everyone else,
expressed the same way.

Functionally it's the office's ambient, harmless haunt — present,
reactive, never threatening, occasionally the butt of the joke (Jazz
cites it as evidence for ghosts being real, Andy blames it for noises,
Olex jokes about haunting the CI server after death).

## In-game mood words

`uhu` (the only one it needs)

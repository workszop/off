# Ghost

**Color:** sky blue `#6AA3D8` · **Sprite:** `assets/ghost.png`

## Look

A simple, round-eyed, friendly outline — designed to be cute rather than
scary, comic relief rather than a horror element. Which is exactly the
problem for him, personality-wise.

## Personality

A joker who is deeply committed to being spooky and just isn't built for
it. He's always angling for the jump scare — flickering the lamp,
lurking by the printer, going quiet then loud again — but between the
round eyes and the soft blue glow, nobody's ever actually scared. The
humans mostly find him endearing, which he either hasn't noticed or has
decided not to take personally.

His whole vocabulary is variations on "uhu" (`uhu`, `uhu?`, `UHU!`,
`uhuuuu`, `UHU UHU`, `UHU!! 👻`), with punctuation, capitalization, and
the occasional 👻 doing all the emotional and comedic work: a plain "uhu"
is idle chatter, "UHU?!" is alarm or surprise, "UHU!! 👻" is a swing at a
jump scare, "uhuuuu" is drawn-out and dreamy. He has opinions about
coffee and meetings just like everyone else, expressed the same way.

Functionally he's the office's ambient, harmless haunt — present,
reactive, never threatening no matter how hard he tries, occasionally the
butt of the joke (Jazz cites him as evidence for ghosts being real, Andy
blames him for noises, Olex jokes about haunting the CI server after
death) and occasionally in on it (the lamp-flicker event is his best bit —
he gets a visible boost at night, when he's more active, faster, and
harder to catch).

    How to Play

    1

    Watch. The office runs itself — desk sessions, coffee runs, meetings, birthdays, the occasional ghost. It follows the real clock: mornings are busy, nights belong to the ghost.

    2

    Tap the glass. Click anywhere on the floor and nearby characters will look up — some will come see what you wanted.

    3

    Peek at someone. Click a character to see what they're up to and how they're feeling.

    4

    Drop a donut 🍩 when they've earned it. Everyone will gather.

    5

    Check the diary 📓 in the panel to see what you missed — the office keeps living while you're away.

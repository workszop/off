How to Play

1

Click a character to select them. A colored ring will appear around them.

2

Use WASD or Arrow keys to walk around. Click anywhere on the floor to make them walk there.

3

Press Escape or click the same character again to deselect.

4

Watch them chat! When characters get close, they'll talk to each other automatically.

5

Use the control panel to pause, change speed, trigger coffee breaks, and more!
